About me

Hey,
I'm Edwin Karimi,

I'm a 22 year old web developer currently living in Nairobi Kenya. I have over 1 year experience mainly in frontend web development.

Education

My journey in coding started back in 2020 during the Covid-19 period. I was in my 3rd year studying B.S.c in Geospatial Information Science at Masinde Muliro University of Science & Technology when it was closed indefinitely.

With all the time in my hands, i decided to jump into coding and i've never looked back since. I'm currently on scholarship at Moringa School, one of the best Coding Schools in Kenya right now, specializing in Web Development.

Job Experience

I have not yet been lucky enough to experience a professsional work environment in any Company or Startup, but i've developed atleast 5 statics websites for confidential clients.

My Love For Code

My love for coding, is inspired by the concept of creation. That's why i chose an Engineering degree in the first place. However, i coding is where i can be my best self. I get to create new things everyday. Things, never seen before. From web designs to new experiences. My love for problem solving also made my love for coding even more.
